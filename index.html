Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our roofing services

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. We work efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards. Commercial projects vary based on square footage and roofing system type.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible financing options through trusted lenders with approved credit. We also work with most major insurance companies for storm damage claims and can help you navigate the entire claims process.

What type of warranty do you provide?

We provide a lifetime workmanship warranty on all our installations, plus manufacturer warranties that can extend up to 50 years on premium roofing materials. All warranties are transferable if you sell your home.

How do I know if I need a roof repair or replacement?

Our free inspection will determine the best course of action. Generally, if your roof is over 20 years old, has widespread damage, or multiple leaks, replacement may be more cost-effective. For isolated issues, repair is often sufficient.

Are you licensed and insured in Florida?

Absolutely! Jenny Roofing is a Florida Certified Roofing Contractor (License #CCC1337890) with full general liability insurance ($2M) and workers' compensation coverage. We comply with all state and local building codes.

What happens if it rains during my roof installation?

We monitor weather forecasts closely and plan accordingly. If rain is expected, we use tarps and waterproofing materials to protect your home. Our team will never leave your roof exposed to the elements overnight.
